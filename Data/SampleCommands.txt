# Sample commands----


# 1.To execute individual test script---> pytest test_Scripts/test_sample.py

# 4.To generate html report-->pytest test_Scripts/test_sample.py --html=Report/'testsample.html'


# 5.To Display allure report-->allure serve AllureReport/testsample.py
